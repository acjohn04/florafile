Diagnosis

Root Rot (Overwatering)

The yellowing leaves, combined with soft, drooping stems, suggest the roots are sitting in too much water and unable to breathe.

medical_services Recovery Plan

  • 1
    Check Drainage Ensure the pot has holes and isn't sitting in standing water.
  • 2
    Hold Water for 14 Days Allow the top 3 inches of soil to dry completely.
  • 3
    Remove Damaged Leaves Prune the yellowing leaves to redirect energy to roots.
